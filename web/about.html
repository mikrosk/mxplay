Welcome to official site of mxPlay!

mxPlay is multiformat (not only) module player for Atari TT/Falcon 030/Falcon 060 computers. It's free software, it has open plugin format, it's skinable, compatible from TOS to the latest XaAES. It features a lot of nice things, to get full feature list download mxPlay or read online documentation.
